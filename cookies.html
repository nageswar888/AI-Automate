Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

AIAutomate uses cookies for various purposes, including:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Preferences cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Marketing cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies:

These temporary cookies are stored in your browser until you close it. They help maintain your session state as you navigate our website.

Persistent Cookies:

These cookies remain on your device for a set period of time or until you manually delete them. They help us remember your preferences for future visits.

First-Party Cookies:

These are cookies set directly by AIAutomate. They are primarily used to remember your preferences and maintain your session.

Third-Party Cookies:

These are cookies set by our partners and service providers, such as analytics and advertising partners.

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually find these settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu of your browser. You can choose to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Be notified when a cookie is being sent
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Delete existing cookies

5. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner asking for your consent to use non-essential cookies. You can modify your preferences at any time by clicking the cookie settings link in the footer.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the cookies we use or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please check this page regularly for updates.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at privacy@aiautomate.com.
