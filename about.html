Our Story

Building the Future of Work

Founded in 2022, AIAutomate started with a simple idea: what if businesses could focus on creativity and strategy instead of repetitive tasks? Our founders, experienced engineers and entrepreneurs, set out to build a platform that makes automation accessible to everyone.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of businesses worldwide, from startups to enterprise companies. Our AI-powered platform has helped our customers save millions of hours and countless dollars in operational costs.
